Overview

PLncDB (Plant Long noncoding RNA Database) is a comprehensive functional plant lncRNA database. In current version, PLncDB contains 1246372 lncRNAs and predicted lncRNA-target pairs in 80 species phylogenetically ranging from chlorophytes to embryophytes. In addition, 13834 transcriptome datasets derived from various samples including different tissues, developmental stages, mutants and stress treatments were used for quantification of lncRNA/gene expression patterns. Moreover, 456 epigenetic modification datasets and small RNA datasets were also integrated to explore the epigenetic regulation of lncRNAs. PLncDB has many unique features such as annotating lncRNAs with a uniform method, solid and extensive information on expression, references, and providing many convenient data accesses, powerful search and download engines. We believe PLncDB is a truly functional knowledgebase and will provide novel insights into plant lncRNA research and benefit the whole research community.
